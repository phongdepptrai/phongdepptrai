### 👨‍💻 About Me

Hi there! I'm a student at the **University of Engineering and Technology (UET)** with a deep passion for algorithms and systems. My work spans across the stack, from theoretical optimization models to practical software tools.

- 🔬 **Research Focus:** SAT/MaxSAT methods for combinatorial optimization, with a special emphasis on **SALB-3PM** (Simple Assembly Line Balancing).
- 💡 **Interests:** Algorithms, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Operating Systems, and Security.
- 🚀 **What I Build:** I enjoy building across all layers—whether it's writing SAT solvers, developing web utilities, creating games, or exploring low-level system architecture.
